Car Talk

Dec 15th, 2010

Well, it’s been a while but something finally broke again on the car.  Repairs on mine and my brother’s Buick are a bit like ‘hot potato:’ it seems whoever’s in possession of the vehicle at the time is responsible for payment.

This time it ended up being a (what I imagine to be) tiny hole in the rear brake line, resulting in a quick and simple fix, for a hefty fee.  Most of the local shops were all backed up so that was unfortunate, but a chain brake shop was able to fix it in only a few hours.

Heading out to Manitowoc tomorrow to see Amanda’s brand-spanking-new nephew; that should be fun and horrifying all at once.  Meeting new faces is usually like that for me, though.  Hopefully we’ll have time to go to the casino so I can blow some more money.

I think my work on fourcafe.com is finally done; I should be getting paid soon for that, thankfully.

Finished problems 8,9, and 10 on Project Euler just now.

Ok, gotta run, Jared’s here.  We’re going to the Comedy Club.

Peace.
