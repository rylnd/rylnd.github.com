An Intimate Look

Jan 13th, 2011

I know I just wrote that last post about the Ruby scraper, but I wanted to take a quick second to talk quickly about what else I’ve been doing with my time.

Mostly, I’ve been reading. This summer I discovered/fell in love with David Foster Wallace, and have read 5 of his books since then. I’d been checking them out from the library, but asked for ‘A Supposedly Fun Thing …’ for Christmas and will probably finish that tonight.

Since I returned from Kenosha I’ve been riding my bike anywhere within reason when I need transportation. I had gotten lazy in December and started driving everywhere, but I’m back on the saddle and feel much better doing so.

I’ve got several things on my Kindle that I’ve been slowly getting through. The details aren’t important, but topics include Ruby, Lisp, regex, Clojure, and a bunch of other things I’m really interested (but severely lacking) in.
